How to Apply:

Let's get you here.


SPRING 2020 APPLICATION TIMELINE

  1. October 15 - Application, including an uploaded unofficial transcript, and recordings
    are due for pre-screened areas.
  2. November 1 - Application, including an uploaded unofficial transcript, and Final Audition recordings are due for non pre-screened areas.
  3. November 10 - Two letters of recommendation are due through application form.
  4. November 15-29 - Live auditions are held at SFCM by appointment.
  5. December 6 - Undergraduate and Graduate spring decisions are released.

FALL 2020 APPLICATION TIMELINE

  1. December 1 - Application, including an uploaded unofficial transcript, pre-screening recording (if applicable), and Final Audition recordings (if applicable) are due.
  2. December 20 - All pre-screen decisions will be released by this date.
  3. January 1 - Two letters of recommendation are due through application form.
  4. January through March - Please see below for audition dates for your area of interest.
  5. March 15 - Graduate admission decisions are released.
  6. April 1 - Undergraduate admission decisions are released.
  7. April 15 - Enrollment deposit for incoming graduate students is due.
  8. May 1 - Enrollment deposit for incoming undergraduate students is due.

MATERIALS AND REQUIREMENTS

  1. Application Fee
  2. Letters of Recommendation
  3. Transcripts
  4. Short Essays and Writing Samples

Application Fee

  1. Non-refundable $110 application fee must be paid by credit card to submit application.
  2. Late applications are charged an additional $50.
  3. Current students and alumni of the SFCM collegiate, continuing education, and pre-college divisions have a reduced application fee of $50.
  4. We accept the NACAC Request for Admission Application Fee Waiver form for current high school students demonstrating economic need. Note that this completed form must come directly from the high school counselor to SFCM via email or mail.
  5. Applicants who demonstrate high need and do not qualify for the NACAC fee waiver or are applying for a graduate or postgraduate program must email apply@sfcm.edu to recieve instructions on submitting an appeal form.
